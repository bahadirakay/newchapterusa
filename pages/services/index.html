Our Services

At New Chapter Coaching & Consulting, we empower individuals, educators, and organizations to build inclusive, resilient, and empowering learning communities.

Our work centers on supporting multilingual learners (MLLs) — including newcomer, immigrant, and refugee youth and adults — and the educators, schools, and organizations that guide them. Through personalized coaching, professional learning, and collaborative consulting, we help our clients grow with confidence, purpose, and cultural awareness — ensuring every learner and educator feels seen, valued, and equipped to thrive.

Services Overview

For Individuals

We guide multilingual adults, graduate students, and professionals to take their next academic or career step with clarity and confidence.

Graduate School Guidance Personalized support with MA & PhD applications, personal statements, scholarships, and assistantships — helping you present your strongest story and next steps.

Academic Skills Coaching Coaching in advanced academic writing, research, and dissertation development — building clarity, structure, and confidence in your scholarly work.

ESL & Career Readiness Coaching Interactive coaching and workshops focused on professional English, workplace communication, and job search strategies for multilingual professionals.

Each partnership begins with listening — so we can co-create solutions that truly work for your educators, staff, and students.
